# Chatroom

## Overview

Chatroom lets the user join live community discussions without leaving the chart. The user can post messages, browse multiple chat rooms, and follow conversations tied to WallStreet.io shows.

## Guides

### Favorite a chat room

1. Click the room name (e.g. "General Chat")
1. In the "Chatrooms" dialog, click the star next to the desired room

### Block or report a user

1. On the message from the user, click the three dots
1. Click "Block User" or "Report"

### Collapse message or user

1. On the message, click the three dots
1. Click "Collapse Message" or "Collapse User"

### Adjust settings

1. In the top right of the chatroom header, click the three dots
1. From the dropdown, toggle "Notifications", "Sound", or "Show Images" as desired
