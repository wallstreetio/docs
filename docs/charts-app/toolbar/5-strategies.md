# Strategies

## Overview

The Strategies tool helps the user filter Wallstreet.io's custom strategies to surface the best ones. Each custom strategy is kept up to date across multiple time frames and may be subscribed to to receive email alerts.

## Guides 

### Subscribe or unsubscribe to a strategy

1. Click on the checkbox beside the desired strategy 
1. A green box will populate between the Filter Area and the Data Area 
1. Subscribe or unsubscribe as desired

### Manage tags 
1. Click on a strategy in the Data Area 
2. The Strategy Stats tab will populate
1. In the "Overview" tab, scroll until "Tags" section is visible
1. A strategy must be subscribed to before it can be tagged. 
1. Add or remove tags as desired.

### Manage Notes
1. Click on a strategy in the Data Area 
2. The Strategy Stats tab will populate
1. In the "Overview" tab, scroll until "Notes" section is visible
1. A strategy must be subscribed to before notes can be added to it.
1. Add or remove notes as desired.

## Reference

### Subscribe to a strategy 
When a user is subscribed to any strategies, they will receive a strategy digest email daily before the market closes that gives relevant summary information. The behavior can be customized from Alerts section of the Account page.

### Tag 
The user may only tag a strategy they are subscribed to. The tags created by a user are only visible to that user. 

### Note
The user may only add a note to a strategy they are subscribed to. The notes created by a user are only visible to that user.
