# Wheel Strategy

## Overview

The Wheel Strategy is a paid Wallstreet.io education program that teaches naked puts, covered calls, and the wheel as one repeatable monthly income system. Lead instructor Gino delivers a live half-day bootcamp plus four VIP implementation sessions across a full wheel cycle, backed by a pre-recorded curriculum, playbooks, a vetted watchlist, and session replays.
