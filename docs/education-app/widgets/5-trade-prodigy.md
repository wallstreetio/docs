# Trade Prodigy

## Overview

Trade Prodigy is a paid 12-month mentorship led by Andy Fetter. Each week, the Trade Prodigy Report breaks down market movements using Elliott Wave, Fibonacci levels, and price discovery to surface high-probability setups and actionable trade ideas. Members get weekly investment ideas, Wednesday reports, Monday streams with Andy, monthly DaVinci lectures with Micah and Andy, weekly market broadcasts, a live chatroom, watchlist, replays, and resources.

Open the program [in app](https://app.wallstreet.io/education-center/trade-prodigy-report).
