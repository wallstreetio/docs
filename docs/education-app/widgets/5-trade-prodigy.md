# Trade Prodigy

## Overview

Trade Prodigy Report is a paid 12-month mentorship led by Andy Fetter. Members get weekly market intelligence built on Elliott Wave, Fibonacci levels, and price discovery, plus live streams, an exclusive trade-room chatroom, monthly DaVinci lectures, and a full archive of past reports and replays.

Open the program [in app](https://app.wallstreet.io/education-center/trade-prodigy-report).
