General
* Only add a guide if multiple steps are involved.
* Don't add guide steps for filling out prompts.
* For guides, don't repeat steps between guides. If you find yourself repeating steps a lot, then add a guide for just that action. 
* Don't use you, use the user. 
* Don't repeat yourself multiple times in different sections. Use "For more see link.", and link to the best section for the information to live. 
* When referring to specific components in guides, use text from the app, quoted it, and write the text as it appears in app, eg click on "ALL STOCKS" or ensure on "Current Stock" is selected.
